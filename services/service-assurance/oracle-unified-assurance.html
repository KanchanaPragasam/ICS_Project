Upgrade Process

Comprehensive Assessment
We start by performing an in-depth analysis of your current environment in order to map the dependencies, customizations, and upgrade issues.
Upgrade Planning & Strategy
An upgrade roadmap, which built on the model of timeline, rollbacks, and testing procedures, is designed by our team to maintain minimal disruption to your operations.
Seamless Execution
We handle the whole upgrade process including generation of back-ups of existing configurations to deploy the new version ensuring data integrity and stability of the system.
Post-Upgrade Validation
All the services, integrations and custom modules are tested and validated to ensure that all these aspects remain as expected in the upgraded environment.
Training & Documentation
We provide updated documentation and training to your team so that they can use the new features and improvements accordingly.

Our Installation Services Include

Environment Assessment & Preparation

Platform Installation
